Privacy

Privacy Policy

Effective July 10, 2026. This policy explains what Arroyo Marketing collects through this website and how that information is used.

Information we collect

When you submit the contact form, we may collect your name, business name, email address, phone number, optional website URL, project notes, referral source, page path, and campaign parameters. Hosting providers may also record standard technical information such as IP address, browser type, and request timestamps for security and operations.

How we use it

  • Respond to your request and evaluate whether Arroyo can help.
  • Route the inquiry to an owner-facing email inbox or lead sheet.
  • Protect the form from abuse and troubleshoot delivery problems.
  • Understand which pages or campaigns generate legitimate inquiries.

Arroyo does not sell personal information or use submitted contact details for unrelated advertising.

Service providers

Depending on the active deployment configuration, information may be processed by Cloudflare or Netlify for hosting, Resend for transactional email, and Google Sheets for lead storage. Each provider handles information under its own terms and security practices.

Retention and choices

Inquiry information is retained only as long as reasonably needed to respond, operate the business, document the relationship, and meet legal obligations. To request access, correction, or deletion, email contact@arroyomarketing.com. Some records may be retained when required for security, fraud prevention, contracts, or law.

Cookies and analytics

This site does not intentionally set advertising cookies. Hosting providers may use essential technical storage and logs. If Arroyo adds analytics or advertising tools that materially change this practice, this policy will be updated before those tools are used.

Security and updates

Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards are used, but no online system is completely secure. This policy may be updated as the site or service providers change. The effective date above identifies the current version.
